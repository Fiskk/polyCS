Resetting a Password

Resetting a forgotten password:

A forgotten CS password can be easily reset if you know your password for the campus network (ie: Banner, Demeter).

If you know your Campus password, go through the account activation procedures again at: http://accounts.cs.sunyit.edu/activate.php.

If you do not know your campus password either, it can be reset at: https://ssl.sunyit.edu/apps/password_reset/.

Resetting your campus password will take 1 day to migrate to the CS network. After that time, reset your CS password by following the link above.
