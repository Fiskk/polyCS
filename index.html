The computing and information fields are enabling the ongoing revolutions in technology and communications that are reshaping our societies. The ways in which businesses, governments, and individuals interact with each other has changed dramatically in recent years and is still changing. The value of the techniques, tools, and problem-solving approaches needed for continued innovations is beyond question.

Computing professionals are iteratively defining and providing innovative new information infrastructures which are acting as vessels for underlying changes in societies and cultures that are extending and enhancing everyone's abilities.

The SUNY Polytechnic Computer and Information Sciences Department offers programs that produce trained scientists and professionals in the computing and information fields. Our graduate and undergraduate programs provide a flexibility which allows students to position themselves in the field according to their own strengths and interests.
